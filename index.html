Key Markets

IDG competes in a constantly changing, highly competitive industry. The mission of the Key Markets initiative is to help teams understand the shifting sands of the countries that make up the vast majority of our portfolio.

The Key Markets Initiative spotlights 8 countries in order to:
  • Discover and promote awareness of client activities in country
  • Understand the in-country strengths and weaknesses of our competitors
  • Promote awareness of and cultivate relationships with local partners
  • Promote the RTI brand, which is rooted in our technical expertise
  • Connect the dots: Capitalize on the complementary activities of our in-country projects, ongoing capture work
